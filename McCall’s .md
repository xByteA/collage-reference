# McCall’s Software Quality Factors (Simulation)

## Given Data
1. Total requirements = 200  
   Fulfilled requirements = 180  

2. MTBF = 90 hours  
   Total Run Time = 100 hours  

3. Memory usage = 4 GB  
   Total memory = 8 GB  

4. Unauthorized access attempts = 50  
   Successful attempts = 5  

5. Training time = 40 hours  
   Development time = 400 hours  

6. Bug fixing time = 20 hours  
   Development time = 400 hours  

7. Testing time = 80 hours  
   Development time = 400 hours  

8. Modification time = 30 hours  
   Development time = 400 hours  

9. System ports = 10  
   Successful ports = 8  

10. Components = 50  
    Reusable components = 20  

11. Coupling time = 25 hours  
    Installation time = 50 hours

# Calculated McCall’s Software Quality Factors

## 🔹 Product Operation Factors

### 1. Correctness
- **Formula:** (Fulfilled Requirements / Total Requirements) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (180 / 200) × 100 = **90%**

### 2. Reliability
- **Formula:** (MTBF / Total Run Time) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (90 / 100) × 100 = **90%**

### 3. Efficiency
- **Formula:** (Memory Usage / Total Memory) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (4 / 8) × 100 = **50%**

### 4. Integrity
- **Formula:** (Successful Unauthorized Attempts / Total Attempts) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (5 / 50) × 100 = **10%**

### 5. Usability
- **Formula:** (Training Time / Development Time) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (40 / 400) × 100 = **10%**

---

## 🔹 Product Revision Factors

### 6. Maintainability
- **Formula:** (Bug Fix Time / Development Time) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (20 / 400) × 100 = **5%**

### 7. Testability
- **Formula:** (Testing Time / Development Time) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (80 / 400) × 100 = **20%**

### 8. Flexibility
- **Formula:** (Modification Time / Development Time) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (30 / 400) × 100 = **7.5%**

---

## 🔹 Product Transition Factors

### 9. Portability
- **Formula:** (Successful Ports / Total Ports) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (8 / 10) × 100 = **80%**

### 10. Reusability
- **Formula:** (Reusable Components / Total Components) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (20 / 50) × 100 = **40%**

### 11. Interoperability
- **Formula:** (Coupling Time / Installation Time) × 100  
- **Calculation:** (25 / 50) × 100 = **50%**
